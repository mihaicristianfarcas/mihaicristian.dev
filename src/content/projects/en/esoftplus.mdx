Most landing pages tell you the product is polished and then ask you to book a
demo to see it. This one shows you instead: as you scroll, the dashboard in
the hero unfolds out of its oval frame, the sidebar slides into place, and
tooltips surface one by one, so the pitch and the demonstration are the same
gesture.

There's no animation library underneath. Scroll progress is interpolated into
a handful of CSS custom properties and the browser composites everything else,
which is why it stays smooth even when you throw the page. Lenis paces the
scrolling so the story reads at any speed.

Built freelance for ESoftPlus, an analytics platform for businesses — profit,
inventory, a daily summary. One long page, one idea, and most of the work in
how it moves.
