I built Atlas for BluePlan, end to end. Engineering teams keep enormous amounts
of technical documentation: standards, manuals, drawings, specifications. What
you need on any given day is one paragraph somewhere in three thousand pages,
and keyword search only helps if you already guessed the words.

You ask Atlas a question the way you'd ask a colleague. It finds the passage,
quotes it back to you, and links straight to the page in the original PDF.

That last part is what I spent the most time on. People act on these answers,
and in this line of work an answer you can't check is worse than no answer at
all. So nothing comes back unsourced.

It's been in daily production use since it shipped.
