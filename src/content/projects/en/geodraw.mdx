Someone describes a screen in a meeting, everyone nods at a slightly different
mental image, and the meeting ends. The wireframe that would have settled it
takes an hour nobody has right then.

GeoDraw makes the description itself the input. You say what you're picturing
and it draws it on a canvas where every element stays a real object. What comes
back is a first draft you push around with your hands like any other sketch,
and you ask for changes in the same conversation.

I worked on the canvas and the generation loop.
